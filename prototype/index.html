    03

    The Bayanihan Mesh

    Communication Without Towers

    When cellular grids fail, ResQLink creates a temporary bridge using Bluetooth Low Energy. Every phone in the community acts as a 'node,' hopping your SOS signal from device to device until it reaches a responder gateway.

    PROTOCOL BLE 5.0 Mesh
    RANGE PER HOP ~100m line-of-sight
    MAX HOPS 7 relay nodes
    LATENCY < 2s per hop
    POWER Ultra-low (days on standby)
